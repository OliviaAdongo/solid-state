Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of real estate services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, lease, or invest, our expertise covers every aspect of the real estate process.

1. Residential Agency

Our Residential Agency division provides end-to-end services for buyers, sellers, and investors in the residential real estate market.

  • Sales and Lettings of Homes and Land: We assist homeowners and investors in selling or letting their residential properties, ensuring competitive pricing and timely transactions. Our team conducts property valuations, marketing, and negotiations to secure the best deals for our clients.
  • Leasing and Tenant Placement: We handle long-term and short-term leasing for residential properties, ensuring that properties are occupied by vetted tenants. From marketing the property to screening tenants, we manage the entire leasing process.
  • Market Analysis & Comparable Data: We provide in-depth market reports, including trends in pricing, demand, and future development projects. By offering comprehensive data, we help clients make informed decisions on when to buy, sell, or invest.
  • Advisory Services: Our team offers tailored advice for clients seeking to expand their property portfolio, buy their first home, or invest in new developments. We offer guidance on the best neighborhoods, property types, and price points based on market trends and client goals.

2. Commercial & Retail Agency

We specialize in matching businesses with commercial properties that support growth, expansion, and profitability.

  • Leasing and Sales of Commercial Properties: From retail outlets to office spaces, we assist businesses in finding the perfect location to suit their needs.
  • Investment Property Analysis: For investors, we offer property analysis services that evaluate the profitability and long-term value of commercial real estate investments. We consider factors like rental yields, property appreciation, and market demand.
  • Market Research & Consulting: Our team conducts ongoing research into commercial real estate trends, providing clients with insights that drive smart investment decisions. This service includes detailed reports on market demand, property values, and future development zones.

3. Real Estate Investment

Our Investment Division provides clients with the tools and expertise to grow their wealth through strategic real estate investments.

  • Investment Strategy Development: We work closely with investors to create tailored strategies based on their financial goals, risk tolerance, and market opportunities. Our team identifies high-yield properties and investment zones with significant growth potential.
  • Market Trends Analysis: Our ongoing research into market trends, economic indicators, and urban development projects ensures that our clients are well-positioned to capitalize on new opportunities.

4. Property & Facility Management

We provide comprehensive property management services that help property owners maximize returns and protect their assets.

  • Tenant Placement & Screening: We handle the placement of reliable tenants for residential and commercial properties, conducting background checks and financial assessments to ensure quality occupants.
  • Common Area Management: We oversee the management of shared spaces in residential and commercial buildings, ensuring they are maintained to the highest standards. This includes security, cleaning, and landscaping services.
  • Maintenance & Property Oversight: We manage all aspects of property upkeep, from routine maintenance to emergency repairs. Our proactive approach helps prevent costly repairs and ensures that properties maintain their value.
  • Rent Collection & Financial Reporting: We handle the collection of rent and provide detailed financial reports to property owners, ensuring transparency and financial accuracy.

5. Occupier Services

Our occupier services are designed to support businesses in finding and managing the best properties for their operations.

  • Portfolio Management: We help businesses manage their property portfolios, ensuring that each asset contributes to the overall success and profitability of the company.
  • enant Relations & Services: We work closely with tenants to ensure that their needs are met and that properties are maintained to a high standard, promoting tenant satisfaction and long-term tenancy.

6. Valuation & Advisory

Our valuation and advisory services offer clients accurate property appraisals and expert advice on a range of real estate matters.

  • Property Valuations: We provide valuations for sales, purchase decisions, mortgages, insurance, and investments. Whether it's a single property or an entire portfolio, our team delivers accurate, reliable assessments.
  • Advisory on Corporate Restructuring & Public Listings: We offer valuation and advisory services for corporate restructuring, mergers, acquisitions, and public listings, helping companies maximize the value of their real estate assets.

7. Urban Planning, Master Planning & Research

Our urban planning and research services help developers and municipalities create sustainable, well-planned communities.

  • Sustainable Urban Development: We specialize in developing eco-friendly urban plans that align with Kenya’s sustainable growth goals. Our team works with local authorities to ensure that projects are built with long-term environmental impact in mind.
  • Project Design & Concepts: From master planning large-scale developments to designing smaller residential projects, our team offers expert guidance on urban and infrastructure planning.
